Help & FAQ

How do I add an item?

Go to "Add Item" page, fill in all required fields (ID must be unique), and click "Add".

How do I edit an item?

From the search page or home page, click "Edit" next to an item, modify fields, and click "Update".

How do I delete an item?

Click "Delete" on any item row; you will be prompted for confirmation.

Why can't I change an item ID?

Item IDs are unique identifiers and cannot be changed after creation to maintain data integrity.

What if I enter invalid data?

Form validation will prevent submission (e.g., negative quantity). Please correct the highlighted fields.
